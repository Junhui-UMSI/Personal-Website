19 century Art Website

Overview

The website project is aiming to provide a online platform for the students in School of arts, University of Michigan. The website contains several useful tools, like the map and Signts&Sounds. The project is lead by the professors of School of arts and School of Engineering, developed by several students from School of Information. The website has now being used in the lectures of School of arts, and is continuously updated every month.

I served as the web developer in this project, my main duty is to develop the website tools of map and Sights&Sounds. The difficulty of the project is we need to generate tons of data from the database and display it on the screen, therefore my team and I decide to choose Django Framwork to develop the website. The MVC model helps us to seperate codes from the data model and makes it easier to transfer the data to the webpage.

Map Tool

The map tool is aiming to provide the mapping of 19 century theaters and palces where historial events in art history took place. The map could zoomed in and it could automatically generate the items one by one on the map.

Sights&Sounds Tool

The Sights&Sounds tool is aiming to provide the multi-media resources about the 19 century art history to the students in the School of arts. In order to make it easier for them to search information, the data was categoried rom databases and genertaed under diffrerent filters on the web page.
